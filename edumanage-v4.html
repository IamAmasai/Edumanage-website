Features

Built for how schools actually work

Not a generic SaaS wrapped in a school theme. EduManage was designed from the ground up for African administrators — offline-first, M-Pesa ready, SMS-native.

📋
Smart Attendance
Mark in 60 seconds per class. Auto SMS parents on absence. Works completely offline — syncs the moment connectivity returns.
💰
Fee Management
Issue statements, track payments, send reminders. Reconciles with M-Pesa and bank transfers in real time. Every shilling accounted for.
📅
Timetable Builder
Drag-and-drop scheduling with automatic clash detection. One click to publish to all teachers and students.
📊
Exam & Results
Enter marks, auto-calculate grades, generate report cards, share with parents — no printer required.
💬
Parent Communication
Broadcast announcements, direct messages, full communication logs. Every parent always informed.
👥
Staff & HR
Contracts, leave, payroll, documents — all in one place with role-based access for every team member.

Our culture

We build with Utu

Utu is the Swahili word for humanness. Every product decision is made with full awareness of the human being on the other side of the screen — not a user, a person.

Utu · Humanness
Engineering with humanity
We do not build features. We build for people. Every engineer is accountable to the teacher marking attendance at 7am and the bursar reconciling fees at end of term.
Pamoja · Together
We build alongside schools
Not above them. Every EduManage team member is assigned real schools they visit in person. We know principals by name. We earn trust slowly, and hold it carefully.
Shamba · The field
Proximity as practice
The Shamba Engineer visits schools regularly, understands real problems, and is the human face of EduManage on the ground — not a ticket system, a person.
