📋
Smart Attendance
Mark in 60 seconds per class. Auto SMS parents on absence. Works completely offline — syncs the moment connectivity returns.

Exam & Results

Our culture

We build with Utu

Utu is the Swahili word for humanness. Every product decision is made with full awareness of the human being on the other side of the screen — not a user, a person.

Utu · Humanness

Questions

Founding Partner FAQ

Everything you need to know about joining our early cohort of schools.

Is my school's data secure?
Yes. We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption. Your data is backed up daily to two separate geographical locations. We are fully compliant with Kenyan data protection laws.
How does the offline sync actually work?
EduManage uses a local database in your browser or app. Teachers can mark attendance and enters grades without any internet. When a connection is detected, the system automatically syncs changes to the cloud.
What is a Shamba Engineer?
Instead of a distant "support ticket" system, every EduManage school is assigned a dedicated local engineer. They visit your school in person, handle your data migration, and train your staff.
What happens if we grow past our current tier?
We'll simply move you to the next tier at the start of the next term. As a founding school, your early-bird discount rate is locked in for the life of your school, regardless of future price increases.
