01 — Features

Built for how schools
actually work

Not a generic SaaS wrapped in a school theme. EduManage was designed from the ground up for African administrators — offline-first, SMS-native, built around the real rhythms of school life. Eight modules, all live.

Live
Attendance

Mark an entire class in under 60 seconds. Auto-SMS parents on absence. Works completely offline — syncs the moment connectivity returns.

Live
Fee management

Issue statements, track balances, send reminders. Every shilling accounted for — reconciled in real time across payment channels.

Live
Student records

Complete student profiles — admissions, academic history, health notes, guardian contacts. One source of truth for your entire school.

Live
Exams & results

Enter marks, auto-calculate grades, generate report cards, share with parents. No printer required.

Live
Parent portal

Parents see attendance, fees, results, and notices in real time. Reduces the calls your office handles every morning.

Live
Notices & announcements

Broadcast school-wide or class-specific notices instantly. Full message logs so nothing is ever lost or disputed.

Live
Staff & HR

Contracts, leave, payroll documents — all in one place with role-based access for every team member.

Live
Property management

Track school assets, classrooms, equipment, and facilities. Know what you own, where it is, and when it needs attention. Built for institutions that manage real infrastructure.

02 — Culture

We build
with Utu

Utu is the Swahili word for humanness. Every product decision is made with full awareness of the human being on the other side of the screen — not a user, a person.

Utu
Humanness

We do not build features. We build for people. Every engineer is accountable to the teacher marking attendance at 7am and the bursar reconciling fees at end of term.

Pamoja
Together

We build alongside schools, not above them. Every EduManage team member is assigned real schools they visit in person. We know principals by name. We earn trust slowly, and hold it carefully.

Shamba
The field

The Shamba Engineer visits schools regularly, understands real problems, and is the human face of EduManage on the ground — not a ticket system, a person who knows your school.
